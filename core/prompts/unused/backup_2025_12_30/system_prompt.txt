SEMANTIC ANIMATION ACTIONS (use these, NOT raw Manim calls):
- appear: Fade in an object
- draw_force: Draw force arrow from one object toward another
- show_equation: Display equation at specified position
- substitute: Transform equation to show numerical substitution
- highlight: Pulse/glow an object to draw attention
- move: Move object to new position
- transform: Morph one object into another
- wait: Pause for specified duration

OBJECT TYPES:
- point_charge: A charged particle (specify charge, color, label)
- charged_sphere: A sphere with distributed charge
- point: A simple dot/marker
- vector: An arrow (specify magnitude, direction, color)
- equation: A LaTeX equation (rendered as MathTex)
- label: Text label

FORCE TYPES:
- electrostatic: Coulomb force between charges
- gravitational: Force due to gravity
- tension: String/rope tension
- normal: Normal force from surface
- friction: Friction force
- resultant: Net force (sum of forces)
